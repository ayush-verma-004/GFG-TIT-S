Our Mission & Vision

We exist to create a dynamic and inclusive coding culture at TIT&S, bridging the gap between theory and practice. Our goal is to empower students with practical skills, mentorship and opportunities to build real-world projects.

  • • Hands-on workshops and project-based learning.
  • • Competitive programming and interview prep.
  • • Industry connect, internships, and career guidance.

What We Do

Workshops, contests, mentorship and community projects — all designed for learning by doing.

Workshops & Seminars
